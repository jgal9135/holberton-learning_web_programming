About the Gal

Julia Gallaway

Bio

The Gal is also known as Julia Gallaway. She is a seventeen year old girl and will be a senior at Bishop O'Dowd High School
this upcoming 2017-2018 school year. She is beginning to apply for college and continues to research schools all across the nation.
Julia enjoys many activities such as volunteering, playing sports, gardening, and of course, cooking! She began cooking when she was little,
standing on her stool, adding ingredients while her mother watches over and teaches Julia how to make the perfect dish.
Julia owes her cooking career to her mother especially, along with her father, grandmothers, and aunts.

Education

Julia Gallaway attends Bishop O'Dowd High School located at 9500 Stearns Ave., Oakland, California.
She will be a 12th grader in the upcoming 2017-2018 school year. Bishop O'Dowd is a Catholic college
prepatory school that offers many opportunities for all students such as athletics, drama, debate, and
a sustainablity club. Julia is a part of or hopes to be a part of all these options. The sustainability club is
one of her favorites! She works with other students to promote sustainability and help in O'Dowd's
four acre "Living Lab." Actually, Bishop O'Dowd was recognized as a U.S. Department of Education
Green Ribbon School for reducing its environmental impact, receiving awards in 2014, 2015, and 2016.
Julia is very proud to be a part of this!

Motivation

Julia had lots of fun creating this website! She is quite glad that she was able to make it based on something that she truly loves: cooking. In cooking,
there are so many different elements that can be found in cooking--the colors, the cultures, the tastes. Food can be an inspiration or
a relaxant for her. Although eating the food is awesome, Julia absolutely enjoys the act of cooking. She turns her music up high and dances around
the kitchen as she makes her creation. Julia wanted to construct this website to be able to share her recipes with the world, enabling others
to feel the same joy she feels when she cooks. Julia hopes you enjoy Chow with the Gal!
